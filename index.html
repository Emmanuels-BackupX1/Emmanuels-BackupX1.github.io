By being able to study the data and looking for where the most accidents happen, we can make navigating NYC safer for everyone. Boroughs

Manhattan

Nearly tied with the Bronx as the borough with the third highest collisions. Car crashes and collisions in Manhattan frequently occur due to factors like aggressive driving, limited parking availability, and a high volume of taxis and rideshare vehicles navigating the densely populated streets.

Brooklyn

The borough with the highest amount of collisions, with much interest focused on the infrastructure.
